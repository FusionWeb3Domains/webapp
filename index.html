Frequently asked questions

What is a Blockchain Domain?
Blockchain domain is an innovative system that provides new levels of security to domain holders represented by a single NFT. A blockchain domain combines two vital ideas– they’re crypto wallet addresses that also function as website domains.
Can I transfer my blockchain domains?
Yes, FSN domains are ERC721 (NFT), you can transfer your domains with the wallets that supports ERC721.
Are NFT domains more valuable than a regular domain?
Depends on how well your understanding of NFT is, will NFTs be of considerable value in the future. You'll never know unless you try it.
Are FSN domains compatible with traditional ICANN domains?
The fact that FSN domains are built with blockchain technology, they are not released by ICANN, and it means that they are not registered in the root directory of the Domain Name System (DNS)
Can FSN domains be traded on NFT maketplace?
Yes, FSN domains can be traded on NFT marketplaces soon.
